Where does your time go?

An interactive data visualization of how the average American spends his or her day!

Select your data

Time is the ultimate resource that we all wish we had more of. Do you want to know where your time is going? Well, we do too! Enter this website. We visualize what the average person spends doing in his or her day in a fun and engaging manner using d3 elements so that we pass softdev. Using datasets gathered from the interwebs, we will display information on things like what percent of their lifetime or a day the average person spends walking, sleeping, etc. This is important because, not only is it interesting to see how our activity accumulate. Data is taken from the American Time Use Survey.
